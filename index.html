Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I choose the right mattress for me?

We understand that selecting the perfect mattress is essential for a good night's sleep. To help you choose, we offer a mattress consultation service, where our sleep experts will guide you based on your sleep preferences, body type, and any specific requirements you may have. Additionally, we provide a risk-free sleep trial period, allowing you to try out the mattress in the comfort of your home and ensure it meets your expectations.

2. Are your mattresses suitable for people with allergies?

Yes, our mattresses are designed with hypoallergenic materials and are treated to resist dust mites, making them ideal for allergy sufferers. You can rest assured that our mattresses provide a clean and healthy sleep environment, free from common allergens.

3. Are your mattresses compatible with adjustable bed bases?

Yes, many of our mattresses are compatible with adjustable bed bases. Elevate your sleep experience and find your perfect sleeping position for reading, watching TV, or simply relaxing with our adjustable base-friendly mattresses.

4. How long does it take for my mattress to be delivered?

Our delivery time varies depending on your location. For domestic orders, our standard delivery timeframe is usually within 1-3 business days. For international orders, delivery times may vary based on your country's customs regulations. Rest assured, we work diligently to deliver your mattress as quickly as possible while ensuring it arrives in perfect condition.

5. What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If, for any reason, you are not happy with your mattress, we offer a hassle-free return process within our specified sleep trial period. Simply contact our customer support team, and they will guide you through the return process. Please note that returned mattresses must be in good condition and adhere to our return policy guidelines.
